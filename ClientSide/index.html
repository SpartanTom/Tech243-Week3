Overview

Hi! My name's Tom and I'm a recent computer science graduate from the university of Liverpool. Before Sparta accepted me into their renowned program I was working as a maintenance gardener in the, unfortunately, very rainy city of Manchester. Saved from getting soaking wet and falling through hedges, I am now training to be a test automation engineer, recently completing the foundation level ISTQB qualification with a mark of 35/40!

While at university I completed my dissertation on the design and implementation of a 2D sim game in unity game engine which is what sparked my initial interest in testing and automation. Writing tests trying to break my game (by overloading the AI system for example) was one of my favourite aspects of the whole process. Although, seeing my code buckle under the strain of keeping track of 100+ AI entities was heartbreaking to say the least!

In my free time I am an avid runner - currently training for a sub 20-minute 5K - along with further developing my game without the looming threat of a 15,000-word dissertation.
